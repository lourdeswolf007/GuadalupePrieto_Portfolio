About Me


Hello! I’m a data enthusiast with experience in collecting, cleaning, and processing data. I enjoy transforming complex problems into clear, beautiful, and intuitive visualizations, bringing data to life through storytelling. I was born in Mexico City and earned my degree in Computer Engineering from La Universidad La Salle. I now live in Boston, Massachusetts, on the North Shore. I completed my MBA at Western Governors University and am currently pursuing a Master’s in Data Science at Eastern University. Beyond my passion for data, I also love painting, cooking, scuba diving, traveling, and swimming. One of my favorite activities is attending aqua aerobics classes at the YMCA.
